About Page

Our Legacy of Care

Founded in 1998, City Care General Hospital began as a small community clinic with only ten beds. Over the last two decades, we have grown into a premier multi-specialty healthcare institution. Our journey has been defined by a relentless pursuit of medical excellence and a commitment to serving the underprivileged members of our society.

Mission and Vision

Mission:

To provide high-quality, cost-effective healthcare through clinical excellence and patient-centric services.

Vision:

To be the most trusted healthcare partner in the region, recognized for innovative treatments and a compassionate healing environment.

Achievements
